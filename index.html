About

My name is Cory Pollard. I am a front end web developer. I began my journey into the field of web development in 2021 as a self taught programmer. I have a great appreciation for good design, an eye for detail and an unquenchable desire to learn new skills. I am one of the crazy few who actually loves CSS, so if you want some artistic flair on your website, I am your man. A few of the technologies I have in my arsenal are HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Sass, Jquery, Bootstrap and React.js. Although I am currently focusing on front end development and design, I aspire to become full stack as I progress through my career.
